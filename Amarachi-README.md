**Planned Mobility for Lagos Commuters**

---

### What We're Building

Komute is a pre-booking carpooling platform designed to solve the daily commute crisis in Lagos. We connect 9-to-5 workers who need reliable, affordable transport with private car owners, shuttle operators, and keke riders who have empty seats on their regular routes — at prices 30–50% cheaper than Bolt or Uber.

This is not ride-hailing. This is commute infrastructure.

---

### My Contributions This Sprint

**💡 1. Idea Conception**
Originated the core product idea for Komute, identifying the gap between expensive on-demand ride-hailing (Bolt/Uber) and unreliable public transport (BRT/Danfo) as the opportunity space. The concept was validated by a viral TikTok by @DarkskinnedElla documenting the daily commute crisis at the Ikorodu BRT terminal — which became Komute's founding story.

**🎨 2. Brand Identity**
Developed the full Komute brand identity including:
- Brand colour palette: Forest Green (#1B4332) as primary, Amber (#F59E0B) as accent
- Typography direction using Montserrat
- Brand voice and positioning: warm, grounded, Lagos-native
- Core tagline: *"Book your seat tonight. Skip the queue tomorrow."

**🖼️ 3. Logo Design (Co-created)**
Collaborated on the Komute logo design, contributing to the visual direction, concept, and execution of the brand mark.

**🔍 4. User Research & Data Analysis**
Designed and deployed a stealth survey to 20 Lagos commuters to validate the product concept without revealing what we were building. Key findings:
- **85%** of respondents would switch to Komute if it existed today
- **70%** spend ₦1,000–₦2,500 daily on transport (20–30% of monthly income)
- **75%** said a verified platform is the #1 requirement before sharing a ride with a stranger
- **65%** want to see who else is in the car before departure
- Identified 3 vehicle owners with empty seats — 2 are immediately willing to list on the platform

Analysed all responses and extracted actionable product and feature insights mapped directly to the MoSCoW prioritisation framework.

**📊 5. Case Study & Insight Documents**
Produced the following research and design deliverables:
- **Survey research document** — stealth-designed questionnaire with answer options, covering commuter pain points, spending, trust signals, and driver motivations
- **Data insights dashboard** — visual analysis of all 20 survey responses
- **Case study presentation** — 10-slide branded deck covering the problem, solution, user research, features, business model, and roadmap
- **PRD (Product Requirement Document)** — comprehensive 14-section document covering executive summary, user flows, MoSCoW features, KPIs, competitor analysis, risks, and roadmap
